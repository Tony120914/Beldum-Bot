Beldum-Bot

Beldum bot consists of a variety of fun and utility commands including 8ball, coin flip, rock paper scissors, tic tac toe, random number generator, Urban Dictionary, getting full resolutions of Discord images, and more!

This is a Discord bot in development. It is developed in Javascript, utilizing node.js, discord.js, request, dotenv, and dblapi.js.

(Certain images used do not belong to me and is used solely for aesthetic reasons.)

What's a Beldum you ask? Well it's a Pokemon! One of my favourites.
